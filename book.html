ABUL KALAM

Dr. Abdul Kalam (1931–2015), also known as Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, was an esteemed Indian scientist, educator, and the 11th President of India (2002–2007). He is often referred to as the "Missile Man of India" due to his key role in the development of India's space and missile programs.
